FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I see results from paid advertising?
+
Most clients see initial results within the first 2-4 weeks of campaign launch. However, significant ROI typically emerges after 60-90 days as we gather data, optimize targeting, and refine creative. Our process is designed for both quick wins and long-term sustainable growth.
What is the minimum ad budget required?
+
We typically recommend a minimum monthly ad budget of ₹30,000-₹50,000 to generate statistically significant data and see meaningful results. For e-commerce brands, we recommend ₹50,000+ for Meta Ads. We'll advise on the optimal budget based on your industry and goals.
Do you work with businesses outside of India?
+
Yes! While we're based in India, we work with businesses across the globe. Our team has managed campaigns for clients in the USA, UK, UAE, Australia, and Southeast Asia. We're comfortable managing campaigns in any target market.
What makes Galaxyfluence different from other agencies?
+
Three key differences: (1) We're ROI-obsessed, not impressions-obsessed. Every decision is tied to revenue outcomes. (2) Full transparency — you see every rupee of your ad spend and the results it generates in real-time. (3) Dedicated strategist model — not a revolving door of junior account managers.
How do you track conversions and ROI?
+
We implement comprehensive tracking via Google Tag Manager, GA4, Meta Pixel, and Google Ads conversion tracking. Every lead, form submission, call, and purchase is tracked back to the exact ad that generated it. You'll receive weekly performance reports and have access to live dashboards.
